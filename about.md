Medema Labs builds simulation software that helps engineers find the best way to manufacture their designs. Our tools sit between CAD and CAM, combining manufacturing know-how with modern computational methods to predict the time and cost of making parts — before committing to a process.

We believe manufacturing decisions should be driven by data, not guesswork. Whether it's choosing the right stock for a machined part, deciding between additive and subtractive processes, or finding opportunities to consolidate an assembly, our software gives engineers the answers early — when changes are still cheap.
